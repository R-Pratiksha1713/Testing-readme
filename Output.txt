#### 1.OUTPUT OF PROGRAM WITH SAMPLE DATA:
The output would display the expected sum.
Example:
###### Input:
Answer the following questions as Yes or No
Is the sum 5 or 9 or 12 or 13 or 15 or 16?
No
Is the sum 10 or 11?
No
Is the sum 14 or 7 or 8?
Yes
Is the sum 14 or 7?
Yes
Is the sum 14?
No
###### Output:
Sum=7
